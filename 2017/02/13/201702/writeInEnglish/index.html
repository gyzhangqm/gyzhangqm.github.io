Write Blog In English

发表于 2017-02-13   |   分类于 Journal   |  

Introduction

It’s time to write blogs in English!

Why?

  1. Living, learning, and programming alone, seldom have I spoken a complete sentence for a long time. In retrospect, my blogs are filled with mathematical formulas. There exists the chance that I would lose the ability of communication if turning a blind eye to this situation. In a sense, writing in English is a way to cultivate language ability while constructing sentences.
  2. More than once have I been embarrassed when I failed to convey my idea clearly, even in Chinese. I cannot express what I’m thinking. Because I either forget a specific terminology or I am illogical and disordered. Writing may get my thinking in order.
  3. People have high aspirations but low abilities. I am no exception. It’s much more easy to think than to do. So if a job is finished, I will write down a blog to introduct the work, the process, the theory, and the troubleshooting. If I haven’t done anything, the nothing will be post. Writing plays the role of supervising and urging my work.
